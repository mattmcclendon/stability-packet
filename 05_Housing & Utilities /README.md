# Housing & Utilities

## Purpose

This folder establishes housing stability and proof of residence.

It supports verification across agencies and institutions.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Lease agreements or housing authority correspondence
- Utility account records (electric, internet, phone)
- Address verification letters
- Move-in and move-out documentation

---

## Governance

Records are preserved to demonstrate continuity and compliance.  
Expired or prior addresses are retained for historical clarity.
