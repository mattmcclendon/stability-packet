# Healthcare & Benefits

## Purpose

This folder documents healthcare coverage, benefits eligibility, and continuity of care.

It exists to reduce friction during enrollment, verification, or transition.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Insurance enrollment and eligibility letters
- Medi-Cal, Medicare, or SFHP documentation
- Clinic assignment notices
- Benefits determinations
- Disability or accommodation records

---

## Governance

Documents are retained to support continuity and verification.  
Medical narratives or analysis are excluded unless formally required.

Privacy is assumed by default.
