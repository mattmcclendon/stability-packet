# Identity & Standing

## Purpose

This folder contains records that establish identity, residency, and standing, and documents efforts to correct misattribution, fraud, or identity theft.

It exists to support verification, remediation, and institutional accuracy.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Government-issued identification records
- Police reports related to identity theft or fraud
- FTC identity theft affidavits
- Address and residency verification
- Consumer reporting agency files (LexisNexis, ChexSystems, Early Warning, Innovis)
- Correspondence related to corrections or disputes

---

## Governance

Documents are retained as records of fact.  
Interpretation or advocacy lives outside this folder.

Accuracy takes precedence over narrative.
