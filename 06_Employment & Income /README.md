# Employment & Income

## Purpose

This folder documents employment status, income verification, and work-related benefits.

It supports eligibility determinations and financial continuity.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Offer letters or contracts
- Pay stubs or income statements
- Tax records
- Employment-based benefits documentation
- Unemployment or assistance records

---

## Governance

Documents are organized by employer or income source.  
Draft resumes or narrative explanations do not belong here.
