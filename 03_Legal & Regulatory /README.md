# Legal & Regulatory

## Purpose

This folder contains formal legal and regulatory records with binding or procedural significance.

It serves as a reference point for compliance, review, or representation.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Court filings and determinations
- Attorney correspondence
- Regulatory complaints and responses
- Official notices with legal effect
- Case numbers and procedural documents

---

## Governance

Only finalized or officially issued documents are stored here.  
Drafts, notes, or interpretations belong elsewhere.

This folder is treated as authoritative.
