# Credit & Banking

## Purpose

This folder documents financial account history, credit reporting, and remediation efforts related to banking and credit access.

It supports reconstruction of financial standing over time.

---

## Scope

Materials may include:
- Credit bureau reports and disputes
- ChexSystems and Early Warning correspondence
- Bank account approvals, denials, or closures
- Secured credit documentation
- Bankruptcy-related financial records, if applicable

---

## Governance

Documents are stored chronologically where possible.  
Superseded records are retained for reference, not removed.

This folder records outcomes, not intent.
